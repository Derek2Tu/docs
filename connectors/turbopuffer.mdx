---
title: 'Turbopuffer'
description: 'Sync embeddings generated from source connectors to any Turbopuffer instance.'
---

<Note>Hybrid search is not currently supported with turbopuffer.</Note>
Any customer with a turbopuffer account can bring their vector database to use with Carbon. Carbon automatically synchronizes content from user data sources (Google Drive, Notion, etc) and the embeddings in your turbopuffer index.

## Instructions
Contact the Carbon team via email (team@carbon.ai) or Slack to share your turbopuffer credentials with us securely.
